About the project

Design de Comunicação II

Synopsis

The project "Sublime is ..." came about after a conversation concerning contemporary art and the subjects that have been worrying artists. During research we came upon a study from Tate Modern on the sublime in art, and subsequently on the difficulty in defining an approach to this subject that raises so many philosophical questions.

We soon realized that each artist was taking a different path in the task of approaching the sublime: technological, natural, transcendent, capitalist (...). In this sense, it was stressed the need to organize a selection of works by different artists in 7 categories, in an ultimate attempt to understand how the contemporary sublime is conceived.

This research work is materialized in an exhibition and in a website. The first one is configured as a relational map affixed to a wall, linking categories and artists. The second one is a compendium of all the information we gathered in order to convey this study of the sublime.
